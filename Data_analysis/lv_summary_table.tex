% TABLE 1 — Root-cause summary
% ─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
\begin{table}[ht]
\centering
\caption{Root-cause taxonomy of lexical-vagueness (LV) prompt mutations that induced a pass/fail transition on Qwen2.5-Coder-7B-Instruct evaluated on LiveCodeBench (52 instances). Each category characterises the linguistic mechanism through which surface-form reformulation redirected the model toward a correct solution.}
\label{tab:root-causes}
\begin{tabular}{p{4.0cm}p{1.0cm}p{1.0cm}p{8.4cm}}
\toprule
\textbf{Root Cause} & \textbf{Count} & \textbf{\%} & \textbf{Interpretation} \\
\midrule

\rowcolor[RGB]{220,235,255}
Variable rename breaks overfit &
14 & 27\% &
Renaming key variables (e.g.\ \texttt{s}$\to$\texttt{seq}, \texttt{n,x}$\to$\texttt{a,b}) prevents retrieval of a memorised (incorrect) solution, forcing the model to reason from the problem description. \\
\addlinespace[3pt]

\rowcolor[RGB]{220,255,220}
Terminology generalisation triggers better pattern &
14 & 27\% &
Replacing domain nouns with generic terms (``graph''$\to$``network'', ``boxes''$\to$``containers'') activates canonical algorithmic templates (BFS, knapsack, set-union) instead of problem-specific shortcuts. \\
\addlinespace[3pt]

\rowcolor[RGB]{240,220,255}
Function rename changes approach &
7 & 13\% &
Introducing an explicit function signature (e.g.\ ``Implement \texttt{gather\_pairs(n)}'') anchors code structure, fixing ancillary bugs (wrong parsing, boundary errors) as a side-effect of rewriting from scratch. \\
\addlinespace[3pt]

\rowcolor[RGB]{255,245,200}
Vague algorithm frees model &
5 & 10\% &
Softening algorithmic language (``find minimum''$\to$``smallest possible'') lets the model select a simpler, correct strategy (brute-force, enumerate-all) instead of an overfitted template (broken DP, wrong greedy). \\
\addlinespace[3pt]

\rowcolor[RGB]{255,225,210}
Input-format change fixes parsing &
5 & 10\% &
Rephrasing the input description triggers regeneration of parsing code, correcting bugs such as wrong token iteration, index offsets, or \texttt{sys.stdin.read()} misuse. \\
\addlinespace[3pt]

\rowcolor[RGB]{255,235,200}
Constraint rephrasing clarifies &
4 & 8\% &
More explicit constraint wording (``divisors smaller than $x$'', ``no empty spots'') disambiguates boundaries the original phrasing left unclear, preventing off-by-one errors. \\
\addlinespace[3pt]

\rowcolor[RGB]{230,230,230}
Other (stochastic / unclear) &
3 & 6\% &
Surface-form change shifts the model's output stochastically onto a correct solution; no single linguistic feature is clearly responsible. \\

\bottomrule
\end{tabular}
\end{table}

\toprule
\textbf{Code Change Type} & \textbf{Count} & \textbf{\%} & \textbf{What it means} \\
\midrule

\rowcolor[RGB]{220,235,255}
Logic bug fixed & 18 & 35\% &
Core conditional or formula incorrect (inverted comparison, wrong operator). LV rewrite prompted the model to re-derive logic from scratch. \\
\addlinespace[2pt]

\rowcolor[RGB]{220,255,220}
Algorithm changed & 14 & 27\% &
Original relied on a wrong algorithm (broken DP, incorrect greedy). LV caused the model to adopt a correct alternative (BFS, DFS, subset-sum). \\
\addlinespace[2pt]

\rowcolor[RGB]{240,230,255}
Cleaner / simpler approach & 10 & 19\% &
LV code adopts a simpler strategy (brute-force, stdlib function) that is correct where the original overly complex solution was not. \\
\addlinespace[2pt]

\rowcolor[RGB]{255,240,200}
Wrong formula replaced & 8 & 15\% &
A specific sub-expression was incorrect (rounding, bit mask, divisor count); new wording prompted re-derivation of the correct formula. \\
\addlinespace[2pt]

\rowcolor[RGB]{255,225,210}
Input parsing fixed & 6 & 12\% &
Parsing regenerated; fixes include wrong token count, index offset, or \texttt{input()} vs \texttt{sys.stdin.read()} misuse. \\
\addlinespace[2pt]

\rowcolor[RGB]{255,245,200}
Off-by-one fixed & 5 & 10\% &
Index or boundary off by one (0-indexed vs.\ 1-indexed return, gap $=2$ vs $=3$). \\
\addlinespace[2pt]

\rowcolor[RGB]{230,230,230}
Variable rename only & 3 & 6\% &
Only identifiers changed; the original logic was sound but variable naming caused a collision or shadowing bug. \\

\bottomrule
\end{tabular}
